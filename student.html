1.Consent for Data Collection: By signing the registration form, the student gives consent
for the educational institution to collect and process their personal information, including
name, address, contact details, and academic records. This condition ensures
compliance with data protection regulations.
2.Emergency Contact Information: The student must provide the name, relationship, and
contact details of an emergency contact person. This information is crucial in case of any
unforeseen circumstances or emergencies that may arise during the
student's time at the institution.
3.Health and Medical Information: Students may be required to disclose any relevant health
conditions, allergies, or medications they are currently taking. This information helps the
institution provide appropriate support and medical assistance if needed.
4.Code of Conduct: The student must agree to abide by the institution's code of conduct,
which outlines expectations for behavior, respect for others, academic honesty, and
adherence to rules and regulations. This condition promotes a safe and respectful
learning environment.
5.Photo Release Consent: Some institutions may request permission to use the student's
photograph for promotional purposes, such as on the institution's website, social media
channels, or brochures. This condition ensures that the student's likeness is used
appropriately and with their consent.
